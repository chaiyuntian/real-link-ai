real-link-ai / gameplay lab

One small video set, many playable ideas.

This repo turns three public clips into multiple browser-playable game loops: rhythm, freeze timing, command decisions, glitch control, and speed-state challenge.

Issue 1 Rhythm Cut

Hit visual impact beats in the Big Buck Bunny trailer.

 Issue 2 Freeze Frame Hunter

Stop the Sintel trailer inside narrow target windows.

 Issue 3 Director Split

Make live command decisions over the Tears of Steel teaser.

 Issue 4 Glitch Chase

Ride chaos zones with timed pauses, boosts, and recoveries.

 Issue 5 Remix Runner

Match section speed targets while Sintel keeps moving.

 Issue 6 Subtitle Saboteur

Choose the funniest wrong caption at the exact wrong moment.

 Issue 7 Meme Sniper

Lock tiny loop pockets to weaponize one clip into repetition.

 Issue 8 Threat Tagger

Tag danger windows like a live surveillance operator.

 Issue 9 Boss Scrubber

Drag through a hostile timeline without touching forbidden slices.

 Issue 10 Emotion Switchboard

Read mood shifts fast enough to keep up with the trailer.

 Issue 11 Narrative Grounding Ops

Drive story state through time-aligned plot decisions.

 Issue 12 Reverse Causality Tribunal

Infer why events happened from reversed footage and plot reveal.

 Issue 13 Tempo Fate Engine

Force the same story into dread, resolve, or collapse through tempo.
Run `bash scripts/download_videos.sh` first Serve repo root with `python3 -m http.server 8000` See `docs/brainstorm-log.md` for the ideation trail
